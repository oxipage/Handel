Seminar - Computing Without Computers 

 

This is a seminar I give regularly around the world to academics, industrialists and investors in technology. The title could equally well contain the phrases “Reconfigurable Computing”, “Custom Computing”, “Hardware Compilation”, “FPGA v. ASIC”, “Beyond Computers” and others - the subject matter covers all these and more. I usually adjust the content to the particular audience and the time allowed – there is no shortage of material!

 

I do not charge for giving this seminar to academics and students, but will usually do so for any other audience unless Celoxica or myself absorb the cost in pursuance of some joint interest. The seminar can also be delivered in extended form as a short course.
