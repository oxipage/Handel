Ian Page - Consulting

 

I have over twenty five years experience of technology consulting alongside my academic and commercial careers. This has spanned many areas including hardware architecture, building algorithms in hardware, hardware/software co-design for embedded systems and the construction and use of electronic design automation tools. A particular theme is in the area of technology assessments for investors in technology companies. These can be anything from simple ‘sanity checks’ on a technology, business or business plan, to a full technical ‘due diligence’ process. Most areas of engineering and technology can be catered for. Individuals from other areas of expertise can be brought in as necessary according to the needs of the project.

 

Fund Raising

I have experience of company fund-raising in technology, having been a principal member of the Celoxica team that raised over $60m in four rounds.  These have ranged from a $400k angel round to a $30m round in 2001 with major institutional and industrial partners. I have also been closely involved with raising several million dollars for my personal research and development work from various grant awarding bodies – some on my own, some with other academic and industrial partners.

 

Design and Implementation Work

A network of experienced individuals and small companies is available that can both design and deliver systems with a significant FPGA component. This includes the design of hardware platforms, programming FPGAs, software construction, and hardware/software co-design. Anything from a hardware core for an FPGA, through an application ported from software to hardware, to a complete system implementation can be quoted for.
